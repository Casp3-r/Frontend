About Our App

A smarter way to know your phone’s true value, maintain its health, and connect with trusted repair & resale services.

Who We Are

Our Phone Valuation App was built to help smartphone users in Nigeria and beyond instantly know the fair market value of their device, understand its health condition, and connect with verified vendors and repairers.

Whether you’re selling, repairing, or simply curious about your phone’s worth, our platform gives you clarity and peace of mind.

  • Instant phone valuation with detailed breakdowns
  • Connect with trusted repair services
  • Smart tips to extend your phone’s lifespan
  • Secure transactions with buyer/seller protection

Our Mission

We believe every phone has value. Our mission is to make phone ownership more transparent and sustainable, by helping users maximize their devices’ worth and reduce electronic waste through repair, resale, and smarter usage.
